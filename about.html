"Keepsake" is a virtual journal application designed to combine the functionality of traditional journaling with the visual appeal of image-sharing platforms. Users can create and personalize digital entries by posting text and images, allowing them to capture memories, thoughts, and moments in a creative and interactive way. Inspired by the Unread Project and BeReal's calendar feature, Keepsake aims to provide users with a seamless way to track, reflect on, and organize their personal experiences over time.

This application is designed for individuals who want to document and reflect on their daily lives in a more meaningful way. The application will take care to ensure accessibility and fufill the needs for different groups, particularly by supporting features like mood tracking and emotional well-being.
