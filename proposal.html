Project Proposal: Keepsake

Problem/Data Domain

The proccess of journaling and documenting memories is a key part of personal growth and mental well-being. In a traditional sense many people journal and document their lives through paper journals, finstas, and private accounts that lack an intuitive and interactive way to pair images and thoughts. Additionally, there is a need for users to look back at their entries in a calendar-like format, providing a visual timeline of their posts.

Proposed Application

"Keepsake" is a virtual journal application designed to combine the functionality of traditional journaling with the visual appeal of image-sharing platforms. Users can create and personalize digital entries by posting text and images, allowing them to capture memories, thoughts, and moments in a creative and interactive way. Inspired by the Unread Project and BeReal's calendar feature, Keepsake aims to provide users with a seamless way to track, reflect on, and organize their personal experiences over time. This application is designed for individuals who want to document and reflect on their daily lives in a more meaningful way. The application will take care to ensure accessibility for different groups, particularly by supporting features like mood tracking and emotional well-being.

App Features

Memory Calendar

  • Users can interact with a calendar to view journal entries from specific dates.
  • Each date will display a thumbnail of the image associated with that day's entry, allowing users to revisit past entries in a visually organized manner.
  • Store data and information that can be used as perosnal preference and even for shared interaction between freinds.

Mood and Reflection Tracking:

  • Users can assign a mood (e.g., happy, sad, calm) to each journal entry, allowing the platform to generate insights into how their emotions fluctuate over time.
  • A visual dashboard will help users track their emotional well-being with graphs or charts.
  • Moods can be shared or stored for each picture, making an interactive platfrom to see the moods of your freinds or track your own mood for personal use.

Interactive Timeline View:

  • Offers an interactive timeline view where users can scroll through their entries in chronological order.
  • Each entry will be represented by an image and short snippet of text, with full entries available upon clicking.
  • An interactive way to relfect on perosnal memories and create new ones in the process
